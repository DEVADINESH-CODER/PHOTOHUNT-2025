Competition Guidelines

Rules & Regulations

Everything you need to know before participating in PhotoHunt 2025

Eligibility Criteria

Open to All College Students

Students from any college or university can participate. Both individual and team submissions are welcome.

Valid College ID Required

Participants must provide a valid college ID during registration and verification.

All Skill Levels Welcome

Beginners and professionals alike are encouraged to participate. Learning and creativity matter most.

One Entry Per Participant

Each participant may submit only one photograph for the competition to ensure fairness.

Submission Guidelines

Original Work Only

All submissions must be original photographs taken by the participant. No AI-generated or heavily edited images.

Technical Requirements

Images must be in JPG, PNG, or WEBP format with a maximum file size of 10MB and minimum resolution of 1920px on the longest side.

No Watermarks or Logos

Images must not contain watermarks, logos, borders, or any identifying marks that reveal the photographer's identity.

Content Appropriateness

Images must be appropriate for all audiences and not contain offensive, violent, or inappropriate content.

Competition Structure

Competition Format

Participants submit their photographs based on the announced theme. Entries will be judged on creativity, composition, and technical excellence.

Photography Themes

Discover six unique themes designed to challenge your creativity and showcase your unique perspective.

🌅

Golden Hour Glow

Capture the magical light of sunrise or sunset. Showcase how warm, soft light transforms ordinary scenes into extraordinary moments.

#LightPhotography
🏙️

Urban Jungle

Explore the concrete wilderness of city life. From towering skyscrapers to bustling streets, capture the energy and rhythm of urban landscapes.

#StreetPhotography
🎭

Emotion in Motion

Freeze the fleeting expressions of human emotion. Capture joy, sorrow, surprise, or contemplation in all their raw authenticity.

#Portrait
🔬

Macro Magic

Discover the hidden world of tiny details. Reveal the beauty in small things - from dewdrops to insect wings to textures invisible to the naked eye.

#Nature
📸

Reflections

Explore the world through mirrors, water, glass, and other reflective surfaces. Show how reality can be transformed and reimagined through reflection.

#Creative
🎓

Campus Chronicles

Document life in your college. From academic moments to social interactions, capture the essence of student life and campus culture.

#Documentary
